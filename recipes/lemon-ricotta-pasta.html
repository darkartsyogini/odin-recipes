Lemon Ricotta Pasta

Another of our favorites to make is Lemon Ricotta Pasta

It's absolutely fulfilling and includes healthy but fatty ingredients. But who doesn't love healthy and fatty stuff, am I right or am I right, haha!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Baby Spinach
  • Lemon
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper
Steps
  1. Cook pasta according to package
  2. Combine ricotta, olive oil, parmesan cheese, garlic, lemon zest, and juice. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Stir well and taste for the seasoning
  4. Leave 1/2 cup of the pasta water in the pasta and stir in the spinach. After 1 minute drain (maybe keep the pasta water)
  5. Add the ricotta sauce and stir in the pasta water to make it the consistency that you want it
  6. Serve immediately

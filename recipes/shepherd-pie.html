Shepherd's Pie

This delicious Shepherd's Pie will fill your tummy up on a cold rainy day

Using creamy mashed potatoes, corn, peas, and of course beef.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion Soup Mix
  • Beef Broth
  • Vegetables
  • Onion and garlic
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Mashed Potatoes

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes and make them into creamy mashed potatoes
  2. Prepare the meat mixture by browning in olive oil
  3. Add the ingredients - onion, garlic, red pepper flakes, Worcestershire sauce, onion soup mix, beef broth. Stir all together and then add the vegetables
  4. Preheat oven to 350F
  5. Assemble the Shepherd's Pie
  6. Bake for 40 minutes
  7. Garnish and serve
